Evolo Skyscraper Design Competition CRP Jakarta

Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia, was originally designed as a water city, where thirteen rivers that cross it fully utilized as a source of livelihood in the city.

Ciliwung River as the largest river that cuts right in the center of Jakarta is the major river that support the life of the citizens, unfortunately this time the river has become a big disaster for the citizens of the city, due to overflowing of water flooded the city. Number of slums along the banks of the river add new problems, namely pollution around the watershed.

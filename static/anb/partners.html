Introducing “Same Day” service on Orchard Road outside the Hotels!

Aunty's Laundry on Orchard Road has teamed up with A&B to offer customers “Same Day” service! Now customers do not need to panic when faced with a tight deadline to clean their important garments.

Aunty’ Laundry started back in 2000 when Mr. Koh took over the shop in the International Building on Orchard Road. He has created one of Orchard Road’s most famous laundries. Mr. Koh’s principle is to offer a range of fast and reliable services to his customers. Aunty’s Laundry can offer Same Day service for dry cleaning or laundry. They can even do a wash load for you in their shop! If alterations is what you need, they can also help you with that. A truly one-of-a-kind versatile laundry shop in the heart of Orchard.

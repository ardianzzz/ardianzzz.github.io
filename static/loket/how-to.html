How to Pay

  • Is the payment can only be done on the ATM machine belongs to BCA, Mandiri and Permata Bank?
  • No, it is not. The payment can be done in any ATM machine as long as it can accept “Jaringan ATM Bersama”, Prima and Alto
  • Is there any additional fee if I pay on “Jaringan ATM Bersama” or Prima ATM machine?
  • Yes, there will be an additional fee if you use ATM Bersama, Prima and Alto ATM machine. Currently their fee is Rp 5000/transaction. However if you pay on the Permata Bank ATM machine using ATM debit card, it is free of charge.

Guinness Arthur's Day (GAD)

Guinness Arthur's Day (GAD) is one of the annual music concert in Indonesia held by Guinness Indonesia from 2010-2013. The event was held in 4 different cities every year : Jakarta, Surabaya, Makassar and Medan. Not less than 20.000 concert lovers came to the GAD event every year.

With more than 20.000 target visitor across 4 cities every year, Guinness needed an efficient ticketing platform that can help them to support and maximize the ticket sales distribution in each city. Guinness appointed their outlet partner across 4 cities to use single platform Loket.com system.

Single Platform Controlling Ticket Sales

Since GAD events will be located in 4 different cities, tracking and controlling the ticket sales has always been a challenge for GAD with hundreds of their partner's outlets accross Indoensia. Loket.com provides Guinness Indonesia with a powerful tool to sell their event's ticket as well as to control sales and commission for ticket outlets under their control.

Digital Promotion Program

As well as controlling the ticket sales, Guinness Indonesia can now access detailed sales and visitor reports to oversee the event performance. Our systems are now fully integrated to link together with their digital promotions . All can be viewed via one dashboard.

Managing entries

With the Loket.com system, GAD also threw away their manual ticket security checking into a quicker, stylish ticket scanner, visitor check-in controller and door entry till giving them absolute control and visibility of events. Not only this, but promoters can also have real time control of visitor numbers and location of visitors.

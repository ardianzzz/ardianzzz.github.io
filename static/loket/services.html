Services

The First White Label Ticketing Platform In Indonesia

Printing Ticket

  • We understand that you want your guest to always remember about you and your event. Therefore we will help you in creating those memories by printing them in the tickets so that your guest can keep them as memorabilia.
  • You may prefer how your tickets would look like. We have a wide variety to choose from, such as the type and the thickness of the paper, the color of the tickets and the type of security for your tickets that fit to your event. The choice is yours.

Redeem Ticket

You don’t have to worry about guests who come to redeem their tickets. Our people will be ready to help you to handle the redeeming tickets process from D-2 to the time of admission to the event.

Ticket Perforation Tax

We understand that you already have your hand full for the event. Therefore we would like to help you in handling the ticket perforation tax so that you can focus on the event.

Ticket Box/Affiliate Partner

  • By using our system, you don’t have to worry about managing ticket sales, even though you use different sales channel or different ticket box. All sales can be centralized in our system.
  • In addition, you can also use our affiliate partner that spread in most big city in Indonesia, so that you can reach more guests.

Marketing

  • We offer a variety of marketing tools to help promote your event to the right people.
  • Any whitespace in the tickets are yours to use for promoting your brand or your sponsor brand or other event partner.
  • It’s a perfect excuse to announce the upcoming event, after party’s event or promotional offer and to encourage your guests to purchase the ticket using our email marketing tools.
  • Using our text message management platform, you can offer your guest a mobile ticketing by sending text message directly to your guest’s mobile phone.

Manage Your Data

  • You will have complete power to control and manage your entire event database including your guest’s data.
  • Any data recorded is stored solely for you and it is not shared with any 3rd party.
  • Loket.com also incorporates a full Customer Management Tool allowing you staff to record any notes, process any refunds and send any receipts.

Venue

We can also help you in finding venue that fit to your event.

Equipement Rental

As part of our services, we provide any additional equipment needed for event management purposes such as scanner, printer, laptop etc for rent.

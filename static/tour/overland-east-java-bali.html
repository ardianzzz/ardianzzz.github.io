Overland East Java & Bali

Eastern part of East Java does not end for a visit , this time we will invited you to join with our caravan holiday packge that program name is “Longtrip East Java + West Bali”. in this trip you will go to:

  1. Menjangan Island (amazing underwater view)
  2. Baluran National Park (as know as avrica van java)
  3. Ijen Crater (one of blue fire in this earth)
  4. Ijo Bay, Pulau Merah Beach (best wave for surving)
  5. and Mount Bromo

Itinerary

Meeting point : Day I at Surabaya (01:30 Am)
Finish : Day IV at Surabaya (16:00)

Day 1
  • Pick up at the Meeting Point
  • Go to Menjangan Island, Bali (8 hours on the road, prepare for it)
  • Back to java for stay in banyuwangi
  • Check-in at banyuwangi backpack hostel
Day 2
  • Week up Call (06:00 Am)
  • Explore “ijo” bay and stone beach
  • Explore red island for hunting sunset
  • Check-in hostel near red island beach
Day 3
  • Wake up Call (00:00 Am)
  • Go to Paltidung (registration post for ijen)
  • Breafing for tracking
  • Exolore Mt.Ijen (ijen crater, Blue fire, milky way, and sunrise)
  • Back to paltuding and directly to baluran national park
  • Explore baluran (Savana Bekol, Bama Beach and Trekking Mangrove)
  • After sunset we will exit from baluran and go to bromo
Day 4
  • On the way to bromo (5 hours)
  • Arrived at bromo and transfer to 4 wheels vehicle
  • Explore Bromo (sunrise from top of bromo, bromo crater, savanna, and sea of sand)
  • Back to Surabaya
  • Droping (16:00)
